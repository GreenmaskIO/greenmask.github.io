# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2025**

## 1. Who We Are

Greenmask is an open-source data anonymisation tool maintained by the Greenmask team. This policy explains what data we collect on **greenmask.io** and how we use it.

## 2. Data We Collect

### Analytics (optional — with your consent)

If you accept cookies, we use **Google Analytics 4** to collect anonymised usage statistics, including:

- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Referring website or search engine
- Country-level location (derived from IP address; the full IP is not stored)
- Browser type and operating system

We use this data solely to understand how visitors use the site and to improve it. **We do not sell this data to anyone.**

### Data you provide voluntarily

- **Newsletter sign-up** — if you subscribe via the footer form, we store your email address to send you product updates. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email.
- **Contact form** — if you reach out via the contacts page, we receive your message and email address to reply to you.

## 3. Cookies

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `cookie_consent` | Stores your cookie preference (accepted / declined) | 1 year (localStorage) |
| `_ga`, `_ga_*` | Google Analytics — only set after consent | 2 years |

We do **not** use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party marketing scripts.

## 4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

For visitors in the European Economic Area (EEA):

- **Analytics cookies** — processed on the basis of your explicit **consent** (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR). You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your `cookie_consent` key from browser localStorage.
- **Newsletter** — processed on the basis of **consent** given at sign-up.
- **Contact enquiries** — processed on the basis of our **legitimate interest** in responding to your message.

## 5. Data Retention

- Analytics data is retained by Google for **14 months** (configured in our GA4 property).
- Newsletter email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe.
- Contact enquiries are retained for as long as needed to resolve the request.

## 6. Your Rights

Under GDPR you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Object** to or **restrict** processing
- **Withdraw consent** at any time (this does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal)
- **Lodge a complaint** with your national data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.
